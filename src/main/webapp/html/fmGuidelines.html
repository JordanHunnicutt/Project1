Here are the guidelines for what constitutes a valid reimbursement:

  • The maximum reimbursement value is $500. If you need to spend more than $500, contact HR in advance of the expense, as it must be manually approved.
  • The minimum reimbursement value is $10.
  • Reimbursements other than travel must be submitted within 5 business days of the expense.
  • Travel reimbursements may be submitted every 10 business days, in arrears.
  • Reimbursements over $50 will only be accepted with some form of receipt. Please provide a link to documentation, or upload image proof with your request.
  • Please fill out all form fields with accurate information. Incorrect details will delay the processing of your request.
